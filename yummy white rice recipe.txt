Ingredients:
- 1 Onion
- 1 Cup of Rice
- 2 Cups of Water
- Oil
- Salt

1. Chop the onion in small pieces.
2. Put oil in the pot and fry the chopped onion until it gets a caramel like color.
3. Put  the water in the pot, and wait until it boil.
4. Add salt.
5. wash the rice with water, until the water no longer get white.
6. Put the rice in the pot with boiling water, and wait for it to dry.
7. When we can't see more water in the pot, we put the cover of the pot and wait until the rice get soft and fluffy.
8. Enjoy it.
